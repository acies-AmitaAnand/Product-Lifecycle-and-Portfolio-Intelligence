# Acies AgenticBus: Portfolio Intelligence
## 10-Minute Conversational Presentation Script & Run of Show (RoS)

This playbook is structured as a collaborative team pitch to senior leadership. **Amita (Lead Presenter)**, **Vyshnavi (Operations & UI Lead)**, and **Jai (FP&A & Data Lead)** are the dashboard builders. They are presenting the new system to the senior executives (the "seniors" in the room) to demonstrate how a VP can identify catalog complexity, audit the underlying metrics, and execute strategic decisions in real time.

*   **Total Duration**: 10 Minutes (Max)
*   **Focus Areas**: Tab 0 (HOME / Executive Overview) and Tab 1 (Portfolio Health Map)
*   **Perspective**: Strictly through the **VP Product Management** role. No persona switching is required.
*   **Tone**: Simple, conversational, professional, and directly aligned with the actual dashboard labels, buttons, and live metrics.

## Presenter Preparation Checklist

Before entering the meeting room, the team should check these alignment points:
1.  **Strict VP Role Profile**: Ensure that you select the **VP Product Management** role at the Welcome Gate when starting the application. Do not use the header profile selector to switch to other roles during this pitch.
2.  **Home Page KPIs**: Confirm that the HOME tab displays the active VP cards: **Total Revenue (₹853 Cr)**, **Active SKUs (99)**, and **Critical Alerts (2)**.
3.  **Alert Investigate Modal**: Practice clicking **"Investigate"** on the Floor Cleaner alert (`a5`) and resolving it using the **"Approve sunset plan..."** button.
4.  **Attention Quadrant Filter**: Practice scrolling and selecting the **"Attention"** quadrant tab under the Revenue vs Performance chart to filter the SKU list for Floor Cleaner and Fabric Softener.
5.  **Email Composer & Decision Buttons**: Verify that the buttons inside the **Decisions** card and the **Regional Bottlenecks** pricing suggestion launch their respective toasts and modal popups.
