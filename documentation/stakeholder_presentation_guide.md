# Acies AgenticBus: Portfolio & Product Lifecycle Intelligence
## Stakeholder Presentation & Agentic AI Guide (Focused View)

This document is tailored for alignment workshops and stakeholder presentations, focusing exclusively on the first three tabs of the **Acies AgenticBus** dashboard (Home, Portfolio Health Map, and Launch Readiness) and outlining the core difference between traditional (Normal) AI and Agentic AI.

---

## 1. Normal AI vs. Agentic AI: The Core Differences

When presenting to stakeholders, it is critical to explain *why* this platform is built on an **Agentic Bus** model rather than standard AI or a chatbot.

| Dimension | Normal AI (Traditional / GenAI / Copilots) | Agentic AI (Specialized Cooperative Agents) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Interaction** | **Reactive**: Waits for a human prompt. You must ask a question to get an answer. | **Proactive**: Runs continuously in the background. Spotting outliers, it alerts you *before* you ask. |
| **Reasoning** | **Single-Step**: Processes a prompt and outputs text or a chart in one shot. | **Multi-Step & Goal-Oriented**: Breaks a high-level goal into sub-tasks, queries databases, and reflects on outcomes. |
| **Action** | **Read-Only / Sandboxed**: Generates text but cannot interact with enterprise systems. | **Action-Oriented**: Connects to tool suites (APIs, databases, email) to execute workflows autonomously. |
| **Architecture** | **Monolithic**: A single LLM attempts to handle all queries. | **Collaborative (The Bus)**: Specialized agents (Portfolio, Launch) coordinate and exchange state on a shared ledger. |

## 2. Tab-by-Tab Comparison & Presentation Guide

### 🏢 Tab 0: Home / Executive Overview
* **Role Focus**: CEO, GM, VP of Product Management
* **Core Value**: Shifting from manual data reconciliation to automated exception-based management.

#### 💡 The AI Difference
*   **Normal AI (Chatbot)**: A user logs in and prompts a chatbot: *"Which categories are underperforming?"* The AI reads the static CSV data and responds: *"Beverages sales are down 5% this month."*
*   **Agentic AI (Agentic Bus)**: The **Portfolio Agent** continuously monitors the sales ledger. It detects that the Beverages category has dropped below its margin target. It autonomously queries the **Profitability Agent** to calculate cost-to-serve leaks and compiles a unified alert. When the VP logs in, a prioritized **Smart Alert** is already waiting, explaining the root cause and proposing a mitigation plan.

#### 📢 Stakeholder Pitch
> *"Today, your team spends hours pulling reports before your weekly reviews. The **Executive Overview** eliminates this prep work entirely.*
> 
> *Instead of searching for issues, the Agentic Bus uses background agents to continuously scan your ledger. When a margin risk emerges, the agents compile a **Smart Alert** detailing the exact root cause—like packaging cost inflation—and draft a localized action plan that you can authorize in a single click."*

---

### 🗺️ Tab 1: Portfolio Health Map
* **Role Focus**: VP Portfolio, Category Manager, Assortment Planner
* **Core Value**: Eliminating SKU proliferation complexity and releasing locked-up safety capital.

#### 💡 The AI Difference
*   **Normal AI (Dashboard / Copilot)**: A user opens a dashboard. The Copilot suggests: *"You should prune low-sales items."* The planner must then manually check supply chain complexity and write an email to the logistics lead.
*   **Agentic AI (Agentic Bus)**: The **Portfolio Agent** monitors the catalog. It identifies tail SKUs that contribute under 1% of category revenue but consume 15% of warehouse resources. It automatically runs a cannibalization simulation (demand transference) and models the exact safety capital released, updating the rationalization cards for the planner.

#### 📢 Stakeholder Pitch
> *"Traditional dashboards show sales volumes but hide supply chain strain. The **Portfolio Health Map** unifies both.*
> 
> *The Portfolio Agent automatically flags products in your 'Rationalize' quadrant. By adjusting the Pareto Concentration slider, you can instantly see how much safety stock capital is released by pruning slow-moving SKUs, backed by simulations showing that customers will substitute to your core hero products rather than leave the brand."*

---

### 🚀 Tab 2: Launch Readiness
* **Role Focus**: Launch PM, Brand Manager, Supply Chain Operations Lead
* **Core Value**: Tracking live launch telemetry to prevent product launch failures.

#### 💡 The AI Difference
*   **Normal AI (Checklist Scanner)**: A project manager uploads a PDF checklist. The AI parses it and outputs: *"Your checklist is 75% complete."*
*   **Agentic AI (Agentic Bus)**: The **Launch Agent** monitors live system telemetry (ERP production schedules, retailer channel listings, pricing databases). It detects that a supplier's packaging lead-time has slipped. It immediately flags the launch date as 'At Risk' and recommends shifting the go-live target to ensure On-Time-In-Full (OTIF) delivery.

#### 📢 Stakeholder Pitch
> *"FMCG launch gates are typically managed using static PowerPoints and manual email follow-ups, resulting in frequent launch delays and inventory stockouts.*
> 
> *The **Launch Readiness Dashboard** provides real-time telemetry. The Launch Agent acts as an active watchdog, alerting you 12 weeks before launch if supply chain lead-times don't align with packaging timelines, ensuring you only greenlight launches that are fully ready."*
