2.2 Why This Problem Matters The functional leaders in this domain face a common pattern: - Data about what is happening is scattered across multiple systems - Decisions are made later than they need to be, often based on stale or incomplete 
information
 - Teams spend time on manual processes and workarounds instead of value-adding work - When things go wrong, they are reactive rather than proactive The virtual lab should make this pattern visible and show what it looks like when AI and agentic 
orchestration
 
change
 
it.
 
3. Scope 
3.1 In Scope - Portfolio health and performance by lifecycle stage - Launch readiness assessment across market, supply, and channel dimensions - Product and SKU profitability analysis - SKU rationalization recommendation and simulation - Market, competitor, and Voice-of-Customer (VoC) signal monitoring - Portfolio and product KPIs 
3.2 Out of Scope - Full PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) engineering system - CAD, R&D, and technical product development workflows - Marketing campaign execution for launches (Lab 1)
