ACIES AGENTICBUS PPL DASHBOARD — PROFITABILITY TREE (TAB 2) DOCUMENTATION

This document summarizes the comprehensive enhancements, structural refactoring, and data-grounding implementations completed for the "Profitability Tree" tab (Tab 2) in this session.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. SOLID COMPONENT ARCHITECTURE & REFRACTORING
------------------------------------------------------------------------
To adhere to SOLID design principles and make the codebase modular and manageable, we extracted the sub-components of the "Profitability Tree" into a dedicated folder (`src/components/dashboard/profitability/`):

- ProfitabilityTree.tsx (Layout Container):
  Acts as a pure layout manager that structures and orchestrates the positioning of the sub-components.

- TakeawayCarousel.tsx (Key Engine Insights):
  Implements an auto-rotating insights slider. Details strategic pain points (Promotional Erosion, Volumetric Dilution, Netherlands Assortment, and Convenience Drag).
  * Refactored to explicitly separate the "So What?" (business implication) from the "Action Plan" (recommended business action) with high-contrast color-coded headers.

- MarginSimulator.tsx (assortment & margin simulator):
  Provides an interactive slider to simulate adjusting Netherland SKU counts and gross margins toward the Austrian benchmark.
  * Added responsive info icons next to Regional Savings and Enterprise Impact metrics, rendering custom hover tooltips that explain the underlying financial formulas.

- ProfitabilityGridTree.tsx (Assortment Hierarchy Drilldown):
  Implements a nested list to drill down through Category -> Brand -> SKU levels.
  * Category: Beverages, Dairy, Snacks, Home Care, Personal Care
  * Brand: BrandF, BrandC, BrandB, BrandA, BrandE, etc.
  * SKU: Individual SKUs with their sales, margin, growth, and volatility parameters.

- PromoErosionScatter.tsx (Promo Margin Leakage Scatter Chart):
  Integrates a Recharts Scatter Plot representing the correlation between Promo Dependency (%) and Margin Erosion Score. Includes quadrants representing "Danger Zone" and "Healthy Zone" boundary thresholds.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. CUSTOM FLOATING GLASSMORPHIC HOVER TOOLTIPS
------------------------------------------------------------------------
To address the business requirement of showing transparency without cluttering the main dashboard dashboard, we built coordinate-tracked hover tooltips:
- Grid-Tree Level: Hovering over any Category, Brand, or SKU triggers a floating tooltip that follows the cursor. Each tooltip outlines a grounded "So What?" and "Action Plan" tailored to the specific node.
  * e.g., Dairy: "High friction, 27.78% low-velocity SKUs." -> "Execute prune of bottom 10% Dairy SKUs."
- Scatter Chart Level: Customized the Recharts tooltip wrapper to render a glassmorphic segment action card detailing the specific recommendations for Keep, Grow, Consolidate, or Rationalize SKU items on hover.
- Simulator Tooltips: Hovering over the Regional Savings or Enterprise Impact headers explains the calculation methodology (e.g. ($38.64% - 38.20%) * sales) in a clean tooltip.
